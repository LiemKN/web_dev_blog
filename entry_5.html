Simplicity

When designing a website, some people think that adding lots of elements and details is good for conveying as much information as necessary. While it is good to convey lots of information on a website through the use of many elements, too much can be easily become overwhelming for the user. Many "noisy" elements and bad use of space are just a few reasons why users will leave a webpage for a different one. Take this for example.

Simple designs are visually appealing and allow a user to navigate the webpage seamlessly. They also allow the user to easily understand the purpose of specfic elements of a page as well as the page as a whole which is the goal of a web designer.
